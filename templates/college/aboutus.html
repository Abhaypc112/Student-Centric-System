ABOUT






SAFI Institute of Advanced Study is a prestigious Higher Education Institution located in the state of Kerala, India. It was founded in 2005 by the Social Advancement Foundation of India (SAFI), a renowned Charitable Trust. The institute offers a wide range of undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs in various fields such as Arts, Science, Commerce, Management, and Media studies. Affiliated to the University of Calicut, SAFI Institute of Advanced Study is recognized by the Government of Kerala and the University Grants Commission (UGC) under section 2(f). Additionally, it holds accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an impressive A++ grade (3.54). The institute has also been granted Minority status by the National Commission for Minority Educational Institutions, Government of India. The institution's primary commitment lies in serving economically disadvantaged, socially underprivileged, and needy students. Its sprawling campus spans over 100 acres of land and boasts a built-up area of more than 2 lakh square feet. Situated in the socially and educationally backward region of Malappuram District, the campus is located approximately 22 kilometers away from Calicut City, towards Calicut Airport. The serene and picturesque environment of the campus contributes to a conducive learning atmosphere. SAFI Institute of Advanced Study is a partly residential campus that accommodates both male and female students, providing them with necessary amenities to foster a healthy cosmopolitan atmosphere. The institute aims to foster total personality development and cultivate talents capable of assuming leadership roles and making significant contributions in all areas of life.
